# Product Overview

## Prince of Kiro - Platformer Game

A 2D platformer game where the player controls a prince character navigating through challenging levels to find the Princess of Kiro. The game features time-based scoring, lives system, collectibles, and dynamic Kiro logo elements that interact with player movement.

### Core Features
- **2 levels** with increasing difficulty
- **Time-based scoring** - faster completion yields better scores
- **Lives system** - 3 starting lives with extra lives hidden in challenging spots
- **Double jump** mechanics for advanced platforming
- **Dynamic obstacles** - spikes and fire traps
- **Interactive Kiro logos** - flee when player approaches
- **Character design** - Prince in white tunic seeking Princess in purple dress

### Game Objective
Navigate through platforming challenges, avoid traps, collect extra lives, and reach the Princess of Kiro in the final level.
